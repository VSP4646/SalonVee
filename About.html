About Us

Welcome to Salon V,

A place where beauty comes from inside our Salon! Violet has been the proud owner of Salon V since 2003. However, her journey as an entrepreneurial stylist started at the tender age of 12 in the city of dreams -New York- where she quickly came into demand as the local hair stylist for both children and adults alike, praised for her talent and passion for hair care.

As the local styles changed, so did her knowledge and experience in different types, textures, and styles of hair. She is professionally licensed for over 26 years and has worked with many top stylists who furthered her experience.

Currently, Violet specializes in the natural hair experience of various hair textures and of various chemical services with a philosophy to continue learning and perfecting her craft. Violet continues to work and manage a diverse and skilled group of 19 stylists who specialize in all new trends of weaving, braiding, cutting, and coloring for all ages and for all hair types.

Violet and her capable team welcome you to discover the beauty within Salon V!
